About MoneyNut

MoneyNut is a simple, fast, and secure budgeting tool designed to help you take control of your finances. Our mission is to make personal finance easy for everyone — without complicated spreadsheets or confusing features.

Smart Budgeting

Track your expenses, set monthly budgets, and understand where your money goes with clean, visual analytics.

Secure & Private

Your data is protected with industry-standard security. We never share your personal financial information.

Easy Insights

Get meaningful charts and summaries that help you make better financial decisions every day.

Our Vision

We believe everyone deserves financial clarity. MoneyNut is built to be lightweight, intuitive, and accessible — empowering individuals and families to manage their finances with confidence.
